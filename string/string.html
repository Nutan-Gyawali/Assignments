Our national flag is unlike any other. Its unique shape sets it apart, and behind its crimson and blue hues lies a profound message. The two triangles represent the towering Himalayas, signifying Nepal’s lofty aspirations and resilience. The crescent moon symbolizes peace, while the sun radiates bravery and an immortal history. Despite being sandwiched between international powers, Nepal has maintained peace—a testament to its friendly nature.
